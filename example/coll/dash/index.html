Exam Date Sheet

Instructions

1.  The remaining time will display in the top right corner of screen to complete the examination. A blink message will be displayed parallaly on the screen when examination time will remain less than 10 minutes.

2.  The examination will be submitted by itself, when timer reaches to 00.00

3.  On the right middle of the screen, the Question Palette is displayed. It contains the numbers of buttons and each button represents a particular question with caption of its serial number. Click on the question number to go to that numbered question directly.

4. The Question Palette displayed will show the status of each question using one of the following symbols:
Grey
  You have not visited the question yet.

Red
  You have visited and not answered the question.

Green
  You have visited and answered the question.

Purple
  You have marked the question for review.

5.  Procedure for answering a multiple choice type question:

(A). To select your answer, click on the button of one of the option.
(B). To change your chosen answer, click on the button of another option.
(C). To save your answer, you MUST click on the Save & Next button.
(D). To mark the question for review, click on the Mark for Review & Next button.If an answer is selected for a question that is Marked for Review, that answer will be considered in the evaluation.
(E). To skip the question, click on the Skip button.
(F).If you currently on question no. 15 and if you go to question no. 5, then after clicking on Save & Next, you will automatically be taken to the question no. 16.
(G). To deselect your chosen answer, click on the button of the chosen option again or click on the Reset button
6. After clicking the Save & Next button on the last question , you will automatically be taken to the first question.
7. You can view the whole question paper by clicking on ViewQP button.
1.  Time for writing answers for the question paper is 2:30 hours and 30 minutes are given for uploading the answers.
2.  Students have to simultaneously upload the answers and not wait till the end of examination. e.g. if student write answer for question no. 1 he has to upload those answers and then start writing next question.
3.  Read the instructions carefully given on the question paper, before attempting to answer.
4.  Use only Blue/Black pen for writing on the answer sheets.
5.  Do not use pencil for answering the questions except for diagrams.
6.  Wherever possible use only A4 size paper for your answer.
7.  Students are advised to limit your words length as instructed in the question paper.
8.  Do not write anything on the answer sheets which reveals your identity.
9.  Do not write your Roll number/Enrollment Number/Student Id anywhere in the answer sheets.
10.  The Roll number/Enrollment Number/Student Id if found elsewhere or any other symbol found that help reveal your identity in the answer sheets will be treated as use of unfair means.
11.  The candidate should write The End after completing writing the paper.
12.  For reporting candidates must use this email id: examination@uttaranchaluniversity.ac.in
13.  Any technical issue regarding uploading will only be handled if they are reported well in time i.e. within half hour on the email provided after the completion of respective exam.
14.  Students are advised to write in such a manner that answers are readable and minimum pages are used.
15.  Image formats that are allowed while uploading answers are *.jpeg or *.jpg.
16.  If in any case candidate upload Wrong/Blurred/Blank images that will be his/her sole responsibility and such cases will not be entertained.
17.  Any other mode for lodging complaints will not be entertained other than email given in point 11.
18.  Candidates can upload multiple files for answering the questions.

Dear Students/Parents,


Greetings of the day!


The UGC in its meeting held on 06th July, 2020 has approved guidelines on academic calendar and examination for the Universities. The extract of the co paragraph of the guidelines is appended below.



In view of the emerging situation related to COVID-19 pandemic in India, it is important to safeguard the principles of health, safety, fair and equal opportunity for students. At the same time, it is very crucial to ensure academic credibility, career opportunities and future progress of students globally. Academic evaluation of students is very important milestone in any education system. The performance in examinations gives confidence and satisfaction to the students and is a reflection of competence, performance and credibility that is necessary for global acceptability.


In the light of the guidelines from the UGC and also other regulatory agencies, the University has decided to follow, following modalities to conduct classes from 10th August, 2020 in respect of students of 2nd year and above.




1. Classes will be held online through ERP.

2. Regular time table will be followed as uploaded on the ERP.

3. All assignments whether objective or subjective shall be given through ERP.

4. The student must login to the ERP as per their time table so as to attend their classes.

5. Attendance will be taken through ERP in respect of the students taking/attending classes.

6. The students must register themselves on or before 09th August, 2020 (Registration date has been extended up to 09th August, 2020).

7. The students are advised to update themselves with required data packs, good desktop computer or laptop with camera, headphone, mike etc., to attend these classes.

8. These modalities shall remain enforce till any other guidelines issued by Ministry of Home Affairs or any other regulatory agencies.

Grafest Advisory
All students have been mailed their E-Passes for Campus Entry for the Celebrity Nights under GRAFEST 2023.
The pass is valid for single entry and will be scanned at GATE No. 2 for all 3 Days.
The link will automatically have your updated pass for Day 1, Day 2 and Day 3 of GRAFEST 2023.
Here are important points that you need to be aware of before planning your visit to GRAFEST
1. ENTRY for students into GRAFEST 2023 will be only through GATE No. 2
2. All students are required to carry their University Identity Cards to enter the premises of the University. No student will be allowed into the campus without valid University Identity Cards.
3. The Grafest E-Pass made available to you in the above link is a mandatory item for all students to be able to attend the Celebrity Night.
4. The pass will be scanned at the Entry Point for the Fest and will be a one-way ticket. Once inside the venue, students are advised not to leave, as anyone who leaves will be denied re-entry.
5. One pass will allow only a single person entry. Please do not share your pass with anyone, as this would mean you will not be able to attend the fest.
6. We advise you to secure your entry into the campus latest by 6 PM on each day, so as to avoid any kind of inconvenience due to last minute rush.
7. In case any of your classmates/friends studying at Graphic Era havent received their passes due to their Email IDs not being updated, they can immediately reach out to their department faculties and get their E-Mail Updated. Pass Issuing will be closed by 3:00 PM today.

SECURITY / BEHAVIOURAL INSTRUCTIONS:
1. No bags (College Bags, Sling Bags, Backpacks etc.) will be permitted inside the campus for the celebrity night. For female students, small handbags, clutches will be allowed. No additional bags will be allowed. Please note and make your arrangements accordingly.
2. The campus entry will be videographer and will be equipped with Breathalyzers and Alcometers - Any student found to be under the influence of Alcohol or any other intoxicant will be denied entry into the campus and will also face disciplinary action. Your security is of paramount importance and we expect you to follow this instruction with utmost sincerity and enjoy the fest responsibly.
3. Any individual found even as much as trying to imposter as a Graphic Era Student trying to enter the campus without permission will be dealt with seriously - Heavy Police Personnel Deployment at the campus will ensure the same.
4. BUSES will operate for the convenience of the students while also ensuring safe departure of the students. Students are advised to ensure their timings, whereabouts are informed to their parents and local guardians.
5. Speedy Driving, Triple Riding, Riding without Helmets, Driving under the influence of an intoxicant, hooliganism of any kind by any individual will be dealt very severely by Uttarakhand Police who are a part of the arrangements of GRAFEST at all times this year.

All of the aforementioned points are meant to ensure that all Graphians are able to enjoy the most awaited cultural fest of the year with utmost ease, with utmost safety and reach their homes safe and sound after the events. In case of any emergencies, requests, issues, the organising team at the venue can be reached out to.
The campus will be equipped with Medical Facilities and EMTs for handling any kind of medical emergencies.
